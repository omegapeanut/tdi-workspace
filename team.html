— Culture

We grow people like we grow projects.

TDI hires for craft and curiosity, then pairs every designer with a senior mentor and an active site to walk weekly. We don't separate the studio from the workshop or the workshop from the site — every staff member is on a job each week.

The result is a team that understands the cost of a detail before they draw it, and a studio that's small enough that every project sees the principals' hands.
