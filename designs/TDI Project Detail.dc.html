Fintech HQ, Raffles Place

OFFICE DESIGN & BUILD
LOCATIONRaffles Place
SIZE12,400 sqft
TIMELINE14 weeks
BUDGET BANDS$1.2M–1.5M
HEADCOUNT220 staff
HANDOVER1 week early
THE BRIEF

A fast-growing payments firm consolidating three offices into one floor — 220 staff, a client-facing front of house, and a brand that needed to read as "established" without losing its startup energy. Fixed lease start, immovable move-in date.

THE CHALLENGE

Landlord approvals and M&E rerouting for a trading-desk zone compressed the usable build window to 11 weeks. Acoustic separation between the open floor and client suites had to pass spec without visually splitting the space.

THE SOLUTION

We front-loaded submissions during design development and pre-fabricated the timber slat ceiling off-site. A fluted-glass spine carries acoustics and sightlines at once — client suites feel adjacent, not sealed off. Handover landed one week ahead of the lease clock.
