COMMON QUESTIONS

Asked before every project.

Do you charge for the first consultation? No. The first 30 minutes are free, in person or online. Bring a floor plan if you have one — you'll leave with a realistic budget band either way.
Can you keep our office running during the works? Usually, yes. We phase noisy and dusty works after hours or by zone, and agree the plan with your building management before we start.
What happens if the quote changes mid-project? Nothing moves without a signed variation order. Our quotes are itemised line by line, so any change is priced against the same list you approved.
Who handles HDB / landlord / authority permits? We do — HDB renovation permits, landlord fit-out approvals, BCA/SCDF/URA submissions where required. It's inside the contract, not an extra.
Step one is a conversation. 30 minutes, free, no obligation — and you keep the budget band we sketch.
