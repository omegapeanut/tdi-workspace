Office fit-out costs in Singapore: the 2026 guide

TDI Studio·June 2026·8 min read Share Copy link

Every office fit-out conversation starts with the same question — "what does it cost per square foot?" — and every honest answer starts with "it depends." This guide gives you the real 2026 ranges we quote, what moves them, and where it's safe to save.

The benchmark ranges

For a CAT B fit-out (your space, from bare or existing condition), current market rates in Singapore fall broadly into three bands, driven mostly by finish level and M&E complexity:

TIERPER SQFTWHAT IT BUYS
FunctionalS$70 – 110Open plan, standard finishes, minimal M&E changes
Mid-specS$110 – 170Custom joinery, meeting-room builds, feature ceilings
PremiumS$170 – 260+Client-facing fronts of house, acoustic zones, natural stone & veneer
The cheapest fit-out is the one you don't redo in eighteen months.

What actually moves the number

M&E is the silent budget-eater. Rerouting aircon, adding server-room cooling or upgrading power for a trading floor can swing costs by 20–30% before a single finish is chosen. Get the M&E survey done before you sign the lease, not after.

Reinstatement is a cost at both ends. Your outgoing space needs to be returned to bare condition — budget S$15–25 per sqft for it, and read the incoming lease's reinstatement clause before your lawyer does.

Where it's safe to save: open-ceiling in back-of-house zones, porcelain instead of natural stone in high-traffic areas, and system furniture over built-ins for anything that might move within three years.
