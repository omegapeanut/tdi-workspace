— 03   Specification Rules

Three constraints we won't ship without.

01

FSC certification for all timber.

Every wood entry in the library carries an FSC certificate — full chain‑of‑custody from forest to delivery. No exceptions for "design intent" or budget pressure. If we can't trace the source, it doesn't get specified.

02

Embodied carbon disclosure.

Stone, metal and glass entries must come with an Environmental Product Declaration (EPD) or equivalent third‑party data. We aggregate this into a one‑page material passport per project — every surface, supplier, and end‑of‑life route, signed.

03

Five‑year repairability.

If a chair can't be reupholstered, a counter can't be refinished, or a fitting can't be re‑sourced in five years — we won't put it in a TDI project. We test every supplier before adding them to the library.
